Community Supporters

The families, individuals, groups and clubs who support our agency make such a difference in the lives of our patients and their loved ones. These dedicated community members donate their time and talent for special efforts such as capital improvements and programs that serve our community in Hospice.

If you would like to participate in a community program, are a corporation interested in becoming a partner, or a small business that might be willing to provide some services to our hospice families, please contact the main office at 805.667.8558 or email us at business.comcareh@gmail.com.
