Job Openings

We're seeking caring, compassionate individuals to join our hospice team in providing care to patients and their families in their time of need. Duties include but are not limited to:

Registered Nurse (RN)

  • Evaluate a patient's physical condition
  • Develop a care plan that addresses any medical needs and social services
  • Coordinate the efforts of interdisciplinary team members to provide appropriate care
  • Perform recertification assessments and complete renewals as required
  • Conduct supervisory visits for LVNs and CHHAs and complete required documentation

Licensed Vocational Nurse (LVN)

  • Follow the plan of care
  • Provide treatments i.e. periodic wound care
  • Order/administer medications
  • Ensure proper documentation of all provided care
  • Work collaboratively with the members of interdisciplinary team to help meet positive patient care outcomes

Master of Social Work (MSW)

  • Assist patients and families in making health care decisions based on personal goals of care
  • Ensure the patient's end-of-life wishes are documented and known by assisting with advance directives, POLST forms
  • Contacting local agencies and/or community resources that may be of help to patients and families (i.e. hospice agencies, Meals-on-Wheels, Life Alert, etc.)
  • Assistance with insurance, Medicare, and Medicaid paperwork
  • Identifying the emotional and spiritual needs of the patient and their loved ones and finding appropriate support as needed
  • Assistance with bridging family gaps to bring loved ones together whenever possible (for example, help obtain temporary Visa's for out-of-country family members)
  • Assisting survivors with necessary arrangements and paperwork after death occurs
  • Assisting survivors in obtaining appropriate grief counseling
  • Identifying other needs of the patient and their support circle and assisting as needed
