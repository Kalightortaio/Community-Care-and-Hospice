Our Services

For several years, Community Care & Hospice has provided end-of-life care, bereavement services, and caregiver support throughout Ventura and Greater Los Angeles.

Our services have grown to meet the requirements of a diverse multi-county area. As your needs change, our staff and services respond with customized care.

Our comprehensive and compassionate services and programs include:

  • Dedicated triage and on-call team to meet unexpected patient and family needs after hours, weekends and holidays.
  • Home delivery of hospice-related medications. We deliver the equipment, the supplies and your medications to make you and your loved ones lives as easy as possible.
  • Specialized and complex care delivered for specific illnesses such as heart failure, dementia, and renal disease.
  • Home visits by physicians and nurse practitioners. We are happy to work in partnership with your medical team, a practice we have been doing for many years.
  • Spiritual care services for all faiths.
  • Services from unique therapies, to an honest commitment from our staff to our patients and families, we at Community Care & Hospice strive to fulfil our mission.
